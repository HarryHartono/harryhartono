Tic-Tac-Toe Game

About the Game

Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic two-player game where players take turns marking a square on a 3x3 grid. The first player to align three of their marks horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins the game.

How to Play

  1. Choose your marker (X or O).
  2. Take turns placing your marker in an empty square.
  3. Try to get three of your markers in a row to win!
  4. If all squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is a draw.

Features

  • Two-player mode.
  • Reset button to start a new game.
  • Simple and intuitive interface.
